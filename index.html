About Us

Precisian Scientific is a trusted supplier of precision laboratory instruments, serving research, education, and industry across India from our base in West Bengal.
Welcome to Precisian Scientific, your trusted partner in food safety, quality, and compliance. We offer a comprehensive suite of services including FSSAI and AGMARK certification, NABL accreditation, food safety audits, staff training and audit preparation.
