Our Consulting Services

FSSAI Consulting (Central & State Licensing)

Our FSSAI consulting service covers both Central and State licensing for food businesses. We guide you through the entire process, from documentation and application to compliance audits and renewals. Our experts ensure your business meets all FSSAI requirements, helping you avoid penalties and operate with confidence in the food industry.

AGMARK Certification

We provide end-to-end support for AGMARK certification, ensuring your agricultural products meet the highest quality standards. Our team assists with application preparation, documentation, product testing, and liaison with authorities, making the certification process smooth and hassle-free.

NABL Accreditation

Achieve NABL accreditation for your laboratory or quality system with our expert guidance. We help you understand the requirements, prepare documentation, implement quality management systems, and get ready for audits. Our support continues through every step until you receive your accreditation.
